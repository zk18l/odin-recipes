Striped Cake

Description
Cake chocolate and milk, striped to create a beautiful pattern and blend multiple flavors.
Ingredients
  • Two cup of Flour.Product
  • Three packets of Vanilla.Product
  • One packet of Baking PowderProduct
  • Two Eggs
  • One cup of Milk
  • One cup of Sugar
  • Half cup of Oil
  • Cocoa Powder
The way to make it
  1. Beat the eggs and vanilla together.
  2. Add the sugar and milk, then beat them together.
  3. Add the oil and beat it.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the flour and baking powder, then add it to the other bowl.
  5. Divide the mixture into two equal parts and add cocoa powder to one of them.
  6. Now, add a little of the milk mixture, then a little of the cocoa mixture, and repeat the process until both mixtures are used up.
  7. Place the bowl in the oven and do not open it before 20 minutes.

After 20 minutes see it, if it's good get it out from oven, wait it to become cold and that's it you have Striped Cake.
