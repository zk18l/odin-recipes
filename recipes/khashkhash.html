Khashkhash sweet

Description

Khashkhash is a delicious and cold dessert that is quick to prepare with few and readily available ingredient.

Ingredients
  • KDD Thick Cream (250g). Product
  • Krem Santi.Product
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk.Product
  • Vermicelli.Product
  • Tea Biscuits.Product
  • Milk.Product
The way to make it
  1. Put the KDD Thick Cream in a bowl.
  2. Add the Krem Santi.
  3. Add the Sweetened Condensed Milk(add as much as you want to achieve the sweetness you like).
  4. Add milk to achieve a good consistency that is neither thick nor light.

Done for the cream, now need to put it with Biscuits in the Pyrex.

Layer cream in the Pyrex dish, then dip the biscuits in liquid milk and place them over the cream in the Pyrex dish. Repeat the process by adding another layer of cream followed by biscuits until you reach the top layer, which should be cream.
Break the vermicelli into pieces, then place it on the Pyrex dish so that it becomes covered with a layer of vermicelli.

After completing all the steps, place it in the freezer to cool, then enjoy the delicious taste.
