Macaroni

Description

Macaroni is a delicious food you can put what you want and make your own pasta and that is a grateful thing.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni.
  • Tomato.
  • Onion.
  • Green Pepper.
  • Black Pepper.
  • Ketchup.
  • chili.
  • Tomato Paste.
  • Water.

This is all you need to make delicious macaroni

The way to make it

  1. We put the macaroni in a boiling water and put it on the stove.
  2. After 12 to 18 minutes we pick up it and remove the water.
  3. We put onion in a pan with oil inside it and put it on stove.
  4. A little later we add the green pepper.
  5. Finally we add the tomato, we wait for little time until it's done.
  6. To made sauce we need
    • Ketchup.
    • Chili.
    • Tomato paste.
    • Black pepper.
    Mix all this things and you git a delicious sauce.
